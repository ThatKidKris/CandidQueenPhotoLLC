FAQ



How many pictures will we receive?

How many pictures will we receive? I Love to shoot Raw and Candid photos in between posed images. With that being said, I usually deliver 25+ images.



What is the best time of day to shoot?

I Particularly LOVE shooting in the Morning Sunrise before the sun is too harsh, or the beautiful evening Sunset.



We don't feel very confident in front of the camera, is that ok?

No Problem!! I will coach you and your family into a few posed images & the rest leave it up to me. I will take as many Candid Photos as I can !



DO YOU OFFER EXTENDED FAMILY PHOTOS?

Yes! I have customized packages for extended family sessions. If your session includes one or two more people than are included in a regular family collection (6), there is an additional fee of $25 per person. Extended family sessions include group images as well as individuals of each family.
